About Us

Alliance University, located in the vibrant city of Bangalore, India, is a premier institution dedicated to providing world-class education and fostering innovation. Established in 2010, our university offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines including Engineering, Business, Law, and Social Sciences. We are committed to academic excellence, research, and holistic development, ensuring our students are well-prepared to tackle global challenges and excel in their chosen fields. At Alliance University, we blend rigorous academics with practical experience, nurturing future leaders and professionals with a global perspective.
